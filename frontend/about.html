About GreenValley University

Founded in 1985, GreenValley University is a premier institution offering world-class education across engineering, management, science, and technology disciplines.

Accredited by NAAC with an A++ grade and recognized by UGC and AICTE, we are committed to academic excellence and holistic development.

To provide accessible, high-quality education that empowers students to lead, innovate, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized university known for research, innovation, and producing industry-ready graduates.
